// HOW TO NOT GET ACTUALLY HACKED

🔗
Hover before you clickCheck where a link actually goes before opening it. The display text and the destination are two very different things.
📧
Sceptical of urgency"Your account will be deleted in 24 hours!" is a pressure tactic. Real systems send polite reminders. Twice.
🔑
Use a password managerIf your password is your dog's name plus a number, a bored teenager can crack it over lunch.
📱
Enable 2FA everywhereTwo-factor authentication means a stolen password alone isn't enough. Add it to email and key accounts today.
📣
Report, don't deleteIf something looks phishy (yes, really), forward it to IT. Deleting quietly helps no one. You might save a colleague.
🧠
When in doubt, don'tThe single most effective cyber defence is a two-second pause before clicking anything unexpected.
